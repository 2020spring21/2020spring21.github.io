Copyright

In the United States, copyright law is administered by the U.S. Copyright Office. A copyright on something original you make is effective immediately. Copyright protects peoples original works from being stolen. In the times of the internet, when so much data is able to be easily accessed, you should learn about copyright so you dont get caught breaking the law.
