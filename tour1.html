The History of DOOM Games

Since its debut in 1993, over 10 million copies of games in the Doom series have been sold; the series has spawned numerous sequels, novels, comic books, board games, and films adaptations. Doom is considered one of the pioneering first-person shooter games, and has been sold and made for many different gaming consoles.
